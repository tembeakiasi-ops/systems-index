## How the Systems Work Together

At a high level:

- **PowerPRO** and **GNATA** act as market-facing platforms
- **LeadFlow** operates as the shared intelligence layer
- Core automation and orchestration are centrally managed
- Frontend experiences are customized per partner or market

This separation ensures scalability, stability, and flexibility.

---

## Design Principles

All systems follow these core principles:

1. **Automation-first**
   - Reduce manual operations
   - Improve response speed and consistency

2. **Modular architecture**
   - Independent system evolution
   - Safe replication across niches and locations

3. **Controlled transparency**
   - High-level documentation
   - No exposure of internal logic or credentials
